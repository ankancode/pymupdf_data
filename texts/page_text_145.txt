408-1 Operations and suppliers at significant risk 
for incidents of child labor
We have not identified any operations as having significant risk for incidents of child labor. Our 
business overall is not at high risk because of the nature of work in the financial services industry. In 
addition, we do not directly source high-risk agricultural commodities, conflict minerals or any other 
raw materials, goods or services in significant amounts from suppliers in high-risk jurisdictions.
